Dear Jonathan D. Fitzgerald,
As you are most likely aware there is a growing agnostic movement within AA that is not without a certain amount of controversy. Toronto and Vancouver Agnostic AA meetings have been banned as not part of official AA. Several lower courts in the U.S. have ruled that AA is a religion. Is this the coming of a religious/non-religious schism?

This fall (6-8 November) a well-subscribed "We Agnostics and Freethinkers International AA Convention" (weagconvention@gmail.com) will meet in LA.

This might make for an interesting article.

I’m moderating a panel “Is spirituality compatible with Agnostic AA?”
The panel consist of:
1. The Reverend Ward Ewing (Keynote Address)
2. Mayra H. (Closing Address)
3. Roger C. (Founder of AAgnostica)
4. Myself

Sincerely,
Vic Losick
